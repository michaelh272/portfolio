4 Year Planner Re-Design

Giving students agency over their academic career.

Brainstorming

Our challenge was to redesign UCSD. How would we do it? 

Needfinding

After deciding on redesigning the 4 year plan, we started by surveying our peers, then interviewing, and then observing. We also collected input from other stakeholders like udergraduate advisors.

Designing

Through our needfinding and affinity diagraming, we came up with a few design goals and went about sketching them out.

User Testing

We put our paper prototypes in front of people and probed them to check for understanding. After their feedback and a few design iterations, we developed high fidelity clickable prototypes with Invision. Tested the effectiveness of these by giving the users a task to accomplish and observing how they went about doing it.
